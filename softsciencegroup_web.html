SoftScience Group, Inc.

Company website redesign - SoftScience Group, Inc. is a small software firm, offering a variety of different skills and expertise. The site design focuses on information structure. The content includes complex and detailed textual information. We had to craft the best solution that would communicate the complicated and detailed information in a logical, understandable and effective way. The site employs Ajax to load external content into a simple tab navigation as well as modal overlays to present the information in a efficient and usable way.

For the past 25 years, SoftScience Group has been combining years of software technical expertise with years of problem domain knowledge in key industries to deliver comprehensive solutions quickly, economically, and reliably to some of the nation's largest corporations as well as small business.
