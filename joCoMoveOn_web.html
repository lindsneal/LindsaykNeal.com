JoCo Move On

As a web designer at SoftScience Group, I worked on a web development project for an organization called JoCo Move On. The organization's main goal for the website was to attract more people to connect and join the cause. The site also serves as a place to promote and distribute important information about events, meetings and news.

JoCo MoveOn is a civic action group of progessives who believe in the power of individuals working together for the common good.
