Mission Pawn

During my time in the web development program, I worked on team with five students to develop a website for our client Mission Pawn. Mission Pawn's existing website had not been up dated in a number of years, so a complete redesign was needed. Mobile responsiveness, Spanish translation, social media, and a Google Map were some of the main functions of the new website. A large portion of the company's target audience uses a mobile phone as their main access to the internet, so mobile responsiveness was key. The Spanish translation feature was important due to the large amount of Spanish speaking customers Mission Pawn engages with regularly. The Google map and social media sections were included to cover the main functions of the website, to provide location and contact information.

The website was designed using the CMS wordpress so the client could easily maintain the website on their own, after the project was completed.
